## Overview

**Project Name:** Nexus  
**Type:** Social Media Application  
**Technologies Used:** Expo, React Native, Firebase  
**Preview:** [Download Latest Releases](https://github.com/ikyawthetpaing/nexus/releases/download/v1.0.0/nexus-v1.0.0.apk)

Nexus is a dynamic social media application built to facilitate real-time connection, thoughtful engagement, and seamless sharing of thoughts and experiences. Developed using Expo and Firebase, Nexus delivers a user-friendly experience, allowing users to stay connected with friends and followers effortlessly.

## Technologies Used

Nexus harnesses the power of the following technologies to provide a robust and responsive social media experience:

- **Expo:** An open-source platform for building cross-platform mobile apps using JavaScript and React, simplifying the development process.
- **React Native:** A JavaScript framework for building native mobile applications, ensuring a smooth and native-like experience.
- **Firebase:** A comprehensive cloud-based platform for developing web and mobile applications.

## Features

Nexus introduces a range of features to enhance the social media experience:

- **User Authentication:** Secure user authentication and authorization using Firebase Authentication.
- **Profile Management:** Personalize your profile with ease by editing details, changing your profile picture, and viewing others' posts.
- **Create Posts:** Share your thoughts, experiences, photos, and links with followers through text-based updates.
- **News Feed:** Stay updated with the latest trends and discussions by scrolling through posts from friends and followers.
- **Like and Comment:** Engage with posts by liking and commenting, fostering meaningful interactions.
- **Follow and Unfollow:** Customize your feed by following or unfollowing other users based on your interests.
- **Real-time Updates:** Experience instant updates with real-time notifications for new posts, likes, and comments.

Nexus isn't just a social media platform; it's a real-time hub for connection and engagement. To explore Nexus firsthand or contribute to its development, check out the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ikyawthetpaing/nexus).
